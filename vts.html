IEEE VTS SOCIETY

About us


IEEE-Vehicular Technology Society (VTS) unites engineers, scientists, students, and technicians dedicated to advancing electrical engineering. We excel in land, air, and maritime mobility services, driving innovation in portable communications, vehicular electro technology, and intelligent transport. Our leadership spans mobile radio, motor vehicles, and land transport, with a focus on 5G, autonomy, and smart infrastructure. Join us to shape a connected, sustainable future in electrical engineering and vehicular technologies.

Vision

To be a global leader in advancing vehicular technologies and electrical engineering practices, encompassing land, airborne, and maritime mobile services. We aspire to drive innovation in portable communications, vehicular electro technology, automotive systems, and intelligent transportation, contributing to a connected, autonomous, and sustainable future.



Mission

Foster collaboration among engineers, scientists, students, and technicians in electrical engineering, emphasizing mobility services across domains. Advance portable communications, vehicular electro technology, automotive systems, and intelligent transportation solutions. Promote research in 5G and beyond communication, autonomous vehicles, and smart transportation infrastructures.
