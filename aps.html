About us

The IEEE Antennas and propagation society supports students who are interested in the analysis, design and development of antennas. The IEEE Antennas and Propagation Society was founded in REC because to the interest of SB students in antennas and electromagnetics and their desire to share it with the other members. The APS Student Branch Chapter provides tremendous opportunities to students for participation in a variety of different activities like webinars, workshops, hands-on trainings, contests, etc.

Vision

To be a pioneer in the field of Electronics and Communication Engineering, shaping the future of communication technologies and creating innovative solutions that address global challenges. Our vision is to empower students with knowledge, skills, and a passion for excellence in electronics, ensuring they become leaders and change-makers in the ever-evolving world of technology.

Mission

To provide top-quality education, promote cutting-edge research, and inspire innovation in Electronics and Communication Engineering. We aim to equip students with skills and knowledge to excel in the field, foster technological advancements, and encourage ethical and responsible engineering practices.
