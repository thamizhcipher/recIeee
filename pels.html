IEEE PELS SOCIETY

About us


The power electronics society are dedicated to fostering knowledge and awareness of cutting-edge power electronics technologies and advancements. Our goal is to empower members, keeping them current and competitive in their professional endeavors. We provide invaluable tools for personal and professional growth, promoting excellence in the field. Join us to access a wealth of information and support, and be part of a global community passionate about power electronics.

Vision

To be a global leader in advancing the field of power electronics and its applications. To serve as a hub for staying informed about state-of-the-art technological developments. To inspire innovation and excellence in power electronics research and applications. To create a collaborative community that drives progress in this critical field of science and engineering..

Mission

Upholding the core principles of power electronics and its educational significance. Keeping our members worldwide well-informed about the latest technological advancements in power electronics research. Providing a platform for knowledge exchange, collaboration, and networking among professionals in the power electronics domain. Supporting personal and professional growth for our members through access to resources, education, and a global network of experts.
